# Content-Strategie

## 4 Content-Säulen
1. **Podcast (sprechstunde.ai)** — Interviews, Live Coding, Solo, Build-in-Public
2. **Build-Sessions** — Proof-of-Work, live bauen und kommentieren
3. **Support & Coaching** — Community-Calls als Content aufbereiten
4. **Unterwegs-Builds** — Signature-Format (z.B. S-Bahn Build)

## Plattformen
- YouTube (Longform + Shorts) — Primary
- Instagram (Reels + Feed)
- TikTok
- Spotify & Apple Podcasts (Audio)

## Repurposing
1 Longform → 5-10 Shortform Pieces
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts
- TikTok
- Highlight-Clips
- Text-Posts / Threads
- Audiogramme
